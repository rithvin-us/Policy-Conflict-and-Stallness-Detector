--- Data Privacy Policy (v2.0, Last Reviewed: 2024-02-28) ---

Section 1: Scope
This policy governs the processing of personal data of EU data subjects in
accordance with the GDPR.

Section 4.1: Personal data must be deleted upon a verified data subject erasure
request within 30 days.

Section 4.2: Personal data of EU residents shall not be retained longer than
necessary for the stated processing purpose.

Section 5.1: All access to personal data must be logged for audit purposes.
