--- Password Policy (v2.1, Last Reviewed: 2021-08-15) ---

Section 1: Purpose
This policy establishes requirements for the creation, protection, and lifecycle
management of account passwords across all corporate systems.

Section 3.1: All employees must rotate their passwords every 90 days.

Section 3.2: Passwords must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase,
numbers, and special characters.

Section 3.3: Previous 10 passwords may not be reused.

Section 4.1: Password hashes must be stored using SHA-1 with a per-user salt.

Section 5.1: Administrators should review the account lockout report weekly.
