--- Backup & Recovery Policy (v1.0, Last Reviewed: 2025-05-30) ---

Section 1: Scope
This policy governs backup and disaster recovery for critical systems.

Section 2.1: Critical systems must be backed up on a daily basis.

Section 2.2: Backup archives must be encrypted at rest using AES-256.

Section 3.1: Backup media must be retained for 30 days before secure disposal.

Section 4.1: Recovery procedures must be tested on a semi-annual basis.
