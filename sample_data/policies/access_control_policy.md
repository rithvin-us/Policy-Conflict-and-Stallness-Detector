--- Access Control Policy (v1.2, Last Reviewed: 2025-01-20) ---

Section 1: Purpose
This policy defines how access to corporate systems and data is granted,
reviewed, and revoked.

Section 2.1: All access to corporate systems must follow the principle of least
privilege.

Section 2.2: Privileged administrator accounts must use multi-factor
authentication for every interactive session.

Section 3.1: Access rights must be reviewed on a quarterly basis by the system
owner.

Section 3.2: Access for departed employees must be revoked within 24 hours of
termination.
