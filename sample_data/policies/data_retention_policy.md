--- Data Retention Policy (v1.3, Last Reviewed: 2023-05-10) ---

Section 2.1: All business records must be retained for 7 years from the date of
creation.

Section 2.2: Financial records must be retained for 7 years to satisfy SOX
requirements.

Section 3.1: Backup archives shall be stored on encrypted media and must not be
deleted before the retention period elapses.
