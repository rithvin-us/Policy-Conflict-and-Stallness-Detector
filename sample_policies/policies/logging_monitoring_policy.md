--- Logging & Monitoring Policy (v1.1, Last Reviewed: 2025-03-05) ---

Section 1: Purpose
This policy establishes centralized logging and monitoring requirements.

Section 2.1: All access to production systems must be logged to the central SIEM.

Section 2.2: Failed authentication attempts must be logged and monitored for
anomalies.

Section 3.1: Security logs must be reviewed on a weekly basis by the SOC team.
